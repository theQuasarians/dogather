Options
When Enabled, all the values of a cookie are visible in the interface.
When Disabled, only the name and the value of the cookie are visible in the interface.
When Enabled, Cookie-Editor will be added to the DevTools tabs. Devtools needs to be restarted to take effect when turning this option off.
When Enabled, Cookie-Editor will show transitions and other various animations in the interface. This is for appearance only.
Selects the behavior of the export button. When "Ask every time" is selected, a menu asking you to chose the format will show up when you press the export button.
Selects one extra element to display next to the cookie name in the interface. This can help you identify quickly which cookie you are looking for.
When selecting "Auto", Cookie-Editor will match your browser's theme automatically.
When enabled, the main button bar will be placed at the top of the interface instead of the bottom.
When Enabled, Cookie-Editor will display some small non-intrusive ads at the top of the main interface. These are used to cover the basic operating costs of Cookie-Editor. Feel free to disable them, but keep in mind that I work on Cookie-Editor in free time as a personal project. You can thank me with a nice review! You can also sponsor me on Github.
All Cookies
Be careful! Operations in this section will apply to ALL sites.
Do not share your cookies exported from here to anyone you do not fully trust. Giving some your cookies this way will give them access to every account you are currently logged in.
Export All Cookies
Header string is not available for browser-wide export since it does not contain information about which site it comes from.
Delete All Cookies
This will delete all the cookies on all the site on this browser. This action is irreversible. Be very careful.
